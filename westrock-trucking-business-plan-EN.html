Why this structure

  • NC as resident state: IRP/IFTA must be registered in state of residence (Charlotte NC)
  • NC vs NJ: NJ raised min insurance to $1.5M as of July 2024 for NJ-base-plated trucks. NC registration allows working NJ route under $750K-$1M federal minimum
  • Wyoming holding: Wyoming does not publicly disclose members. NC requires public disclosure. Wyoming as member of NC LLC = NC records show only "Wyoming LLC", names hidden
  • Nominee Manager: signs public documents (broker agreements, contracts) on behalf of NC LLC. Real control (bank, operations) stays with partners via private Operating Agreement
  • What it does NOT hide: bank (KYC), insurance underwriting (SSN driver record), IRS, FMCSA enforcement actions. This protects from competitors and public records, not from government

📚 Reserve Fund and Working Capital — what they are
🛠️ Reserve Fund ($15k V1 / $25k V2) — "breakdown buffer"

Separate bank account, untouchable reserve for emergency repairs.

Why needed (used truck 350-450k mi):
• Engine overhaul DD15/X15: $18-25k
• Transmission rebuild: $8-15k
• Turbo: $4-6k
• EGR cooler + DEF: $3-8k
• DPF system: $2.5-5k
• Drive tires set (18 pcs): $8-10k every 150k mi

Without reserve: 1 breakdown = truck sits 2-4 wks in shop = you don't deliver 8-15 r/t = -$12-22k revenue + still paying driver/insurance/loan → default → bank takes truck → business dead.

Rule: +$250-500/mo accumulation (built into Reserve fund line). By end of year $18-30k. Never use for operating cash flow.
💰 Working Capital ($40k V1 / $60k V2) — "AR bridge"

Working capital to cover expenses while the customer pays with delay.

Why: Westrock pays net-30 or net-45 (30-45 days after invoice). But expenses are daily:
• Diesel ~$2,800/wk (V1) or ~$4,800/wk (V2)
• Drivers salary $4-7k every 2 wks
• Insurance $1,400-2,800/mo
• Loans $2-3.7k/mo (if financed)

Cash flow timeline:
• Month 1: working, invoices sent. Received: $0. Spent: $20-37k.
• Month 2: first payments come in, continue working.
• Need buffer ~1.5-2 mo of operating expenses = $40k V1 / $60k V2.

Alternative — factoring: OTR Capital / RTS pay immediately for 2-3% fee. On $75k/mo = $1.5-2.25k/mo = $18-27k/year. Strategy: first 3-6 mo factoring (no Westrock credit history yet), then direct net-30.
